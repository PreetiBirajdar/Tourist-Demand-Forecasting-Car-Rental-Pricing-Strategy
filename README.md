# 🚗 Tourist Demand Forecasting & Car Rental Pricing Strategy

## Project Overview
This case study explores tourist demand patterns and car rental pricing strategy to support revenue-focused business decisions. Using SQL, Excel, and Tableau, the analysis examines seasonality, booking demand, pricing trends, and tourism activity to identify opportunities for improved pricing and capacity planning.

## Business Problem
Car rental companies operate in a demand-sensitive market where tourism trends, seasonal fluctuations, and pricing decisions directly affect revenue and vehicle utilization. Without a structured demand and pricing analysis, businesses may underprice during peak periods, overprice during slower periods, or misallocate fleet availability.

This project addresses the need to forecast tourist demand and evaluate pricing opportunities that can support stronger revenue management and more informed operational planning.

## Objectives
- Analyze tourist demand trends and seasonality
- Evaluate car rental pricing patterns across demand periods
- Identify high-demand and low-demand windows
- Support data-driven pricing and revenue strategy
- Present findings through an interactive Tableau dashboard

## Tools Used
- **Excel** – Initial data review, cleaning, validation, and preparation
- **SQL** – Advanced analysis using joins, CTEs, window functions, ranking, segmentation, and pricing opportunity logic
- **Tableau** – Dashboard design, demand trend visualization, pricing analysis, and storytelling
